Theory: Double Integration

Double integration is a way to integrate over a two-dimensional area. It is used to calculate the volume under a surface, defined by z = f(x, y).


The general format is:

$$\int_{x_1}^{x_2} \int_{y_1}^{y_2} f(x,y) \,dy \,dx$$

We solve the inner integral first (usually with respect to y), treating x as a constant. Then, we solve the outer integral with respect to x.
